# ⚙️ Operations Dashboard

![Operations Dashboard](PT%20vận%20hành.png)

---

## Business Objective

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of daily business operations by monitoring class performance, revenue, customer activity, occupancy, and operational KPIs. It enables managers to optimize resource allocation, improve class utilization, and support data-driven operational decisions across branches.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Revenue per Class
- ARPS
- ARPE
- Total Active Members (TAM)
- Show Rate
- Show per Coach
- Toss per Day
- Customer Classification
- Class Occupancy
- Renewal Conversion
- Daily Revenue Performance

---

## Business Insights

- Monitor operational performance across branches and reporting periods.
- Compare revenue performance by class type and time slot.
- Evaluate customer distribution across membership categories.
- Identify underutilized time slots through occupancy heatmaps.
- Track daily operational KPIs to support timely management decisions.

---

## Recommendations

- Optimize class schedules based on occupancy and demand patterns.
- Increase utilization of low-performing time slots through targeted promotions.
- Monitor daily operational KPIs to detect abnormal performance changes early.
- Reallocate coaching resources based on customer demand and class utilization.
- Improve customer retention by focusing on high-value member segments.

---

## Business Value

This dashboard helps operations managers improve resource utilization, maximize class occupancy, monitor operational efficiency, and support strategic planning through centralized business reporting.
