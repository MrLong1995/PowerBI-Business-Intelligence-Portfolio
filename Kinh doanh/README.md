# 📊 Business Performance Dashboard

![Business Performance Dashboard](business-performance-dashboard.png)

---

## Business Objective

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by integrating sales, marketing, customer acquisition, revenue, and operational KPIs into a single reporting solution. It enables management to monitor business health, identify performance bottlenecks, evaluate branch performance, and support data-driven strategic decisions.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Marketing Revenue (MOL)
- Business Revenue (BR)
- Renewal Revenue
- Revenue per MOL
- Revenue per C3
- ARPU
- Customer Acquisition
- Marketing Funnel (C3 → L8)
- Weekly Business Performance
- Branch Performance
- Business KPI Achievement

---

## Technical Highlights

### Data Architecture
- Star Schema Data Model
- DirectQuery connection to the production database

### Power BI Development
- 50+ DAX Measures
- Power Query
- Time Intelligence
- Interactive Dashboard
- Dynamic KPI Cards
- Funnel Analysis
- Conditional Formatting
- Custom KPI Calculations

### Deployment
- Published to Power BI Service
- Scheduled Data Refresh
- Row-Level Security (RLS)

---

## Business Insights

- Monitor overall business performance through integrated operational, sales, and marketing KPIs.
- Analyze customer conversion from C3 to L8 to identify bottlenecks in the acquisition funnel.
- Compare revenue contribution across marketing channels, branches, and business units.
- Evaluate weekly revenue performance and marketing efficiency using customized KPI metrics.
- Track ARPU, revenue per customer, and renewal performance to support sustainable business growth.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve conversion rates at key stages of the customer journey to maximize revenue.
- Allocate marketing resources toward channels with higher revenue contribution and conversion efficiency.
- Monitor weekly KPI trends to identify performance issues at an early stage.
- Optimize branch operations by benchmarking high-performing locations.
- Strengthen customer retention and renewal strategies to improve long-term business value.

---

## Business Value

This dashboard provides management with a centralized view of business performance, enabling faster decision-making, improved operational visibility, and more effective monitoring of commercial KPIs across the organization.
