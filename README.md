# 📊 Power BI Business Intelligence Portfolio

A collection of Business Intelligence dashboards developed with Power BI to support business monitoring and data-driven decision making.

> **Note:** All dashboards use anonymized and scaled data for portfolio purposes. Business logic, visualizations and KPIs remain representative of the original solutions.

---

#  Tools & Technologies

- Power BI Desktop
- DAX
- Power Query
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)
- Interactive Dashboard Design

---

#  Dashboards

##  1. Operations Dashboard

Monitor overall business performance, including revenue, occupancy, customer growth and operational KPIs.

---

##  2. Coach Performance Dashboard

Analyze coach performance through revenue, retention, renewal and customer engagement metrics.

---

##  3. Marketing Performance Dashboard

Track marketing funnel performance, customer acquisition and conversion across different channels.

---

##  4. Sales Performance Dashboard

Monitor sales performance, revenue trends and customer acquisition to support business growth.

---
